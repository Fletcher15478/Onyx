FAQ

Questions about renting or management?

Straightforward answers for Morgantown renters and property owners. For anything not covered here, reach out through the contact form above.

How do I schedule a showing for a property?

Start on the Available Rentals section to view current listings. From there you can follow the prompts in the Buildium listing to request a showing or begin your application.

What is required to apply for a Morgantown rental?

Applications are completed online through the Buildium portal. You will typically provide proof of income, rental history, identification, and consent for background and credit screening.

How quickly are maintenance requests handled?

Emergency issues are prioritized immediately. Non-emergency maintenance submitted through the resident portal is typically acknowledged the same business day and scheduled as quickly as possible.

Do you offer full-service property management for single rentals and portfolios?

Yes. Onyx Property Management works with individual owners and investors, providing leasing, rent collection, maintenance coordination, and detailed monthly reporting for properties in and around Morgantown, WV.
